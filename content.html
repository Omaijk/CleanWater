Our Goal 🎯

Our campaign aims to reduce untreated wastewater in Indonesia by raising awareness, encouraging responsible waste disposal, and supporting better sanitation systems. We want cleaner rivers, healthier communities, and a safer environment for future generations.

How We Achieve It

Step 1: Awareness 📢
Educate people about the dangers of sewage pollution through social media, schools, and campaigns.

Step 2: Community Action 🤝
Encourage communities to participate in clean-up events and protect local water sources.

Step 3: Better Habits 🚿
Promote proper waste disposal and reduce harmful chemicals going into drains.

Step 4: Support Solutions 🏗️
Advocate for improved sewage treatment systems and infrastructure development.
